Kadva Patel Samaj

The Kadva Patel Samaj in Dwarka is a modern sanctuary for community and pilgrimage. This purpose-built complex, inaugurated in 2018, masterfully blends tradition with contemporary service. At its core is a fully equipped guest house, the ‘Atithi Gruh,’ offering clean, spacious rooms and dedicated dormitories, providing dignified and affordable accommodation for all visitors and family groups.

Its serene location, just moments from the sacred seashore, offers guests a deeply spiritual environment. More than a meeting hall, it is a vital home away from home that actively strengthens community bonds by serving the practical needs of its members with foresight and hospitality.
